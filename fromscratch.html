Peyton Manning Career Overview

  • 5 Cool things to know about Peyton Manning
    1. Peyton Manning was nicknamed "The Sheriff" because he always seemed to be in control of the game.
    2. He was able to command his teammates on what routes to run, and he knew what plays to call.
    3. He finished his career with the highest number of touchdown passes thrown in a regular season.
    4. He was able to defeat every team in the NFL (Only one quarterback was able to do that before him).
    5. Peyton Manning suffered a terrible neck injury that many thought he could never recover from, but he went on to win multiple MVP awards and a Super Bowl.
  • What made him so special?
    Peyton Manning spent more time studying game film than any of his opponents. He was never the most athletic athlete, but he knew his strengths and played to them.
